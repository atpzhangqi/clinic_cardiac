Summary of Left Main Coronary Artery
❑Origin
Upper portion pf left aortic sinus just below the sinotubular ridge. Typically 0-10mm in length. Rarely no LMCA(separate origins). 
❑Optimal Views
LAO caudal and cranial; AP-caudal, cranial or flat.
