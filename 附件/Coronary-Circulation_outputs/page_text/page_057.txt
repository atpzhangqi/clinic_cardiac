groove and the lower portion of the interatrial septum. The arteriogram shows that atrial branches (B) originating from the proximal left
circumflex artery and the RCA connect and form an anastomotic network (N). It also shows the contribution of the atrioventricular node (AVN)
artery to the network. Note that the atrial branches shown The authors magnified this figure to better demonstrate the network and also because the
